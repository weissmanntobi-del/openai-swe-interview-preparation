# Seven-Day Preparation Sprint

## Day 1: Role calibration

Read the current job description and official guidance. Select a role map. Record a baseline coding answer and one behavioral answer.

## Day 2: Practical coding

Attempt questions 1, 2, and 4. Review invariants, testing, complexity, and communication.

## Day 3: Distributed systems

Attempt questions 11, 12, 14, and 17. Focus on duplicates, retries, leases, fairness, and overload.

## Day 4: System design

Complete one 45-minute design from questions 19–28. Redo it after adding a failure or scale constraint.

## Day 5: AI platform

Complete questions 29, 31, 32, and 34. Make quality, latency, cost, privacy, and rollback explicit.

## Day 6: Incidents and behavioral evidence

Complete two incident scenarios and prepare five behavioral stories with measurable results.

## Day 7: Mock loop

Run:
- 45-minute coding mock;
- 45-minute design mock;
- 30-minute behavioral mock;
- scorecard review;
- targeted repair of the two weakest dimensions.

Stop collecting new material. Improve execution.
