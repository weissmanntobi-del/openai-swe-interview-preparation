# Contributing

Contributions should improve educational quality without presenting confidential interview information.

## Accepted

- original questions;
- better constraints and follow-ups;
- clearer evaluation signals;
- original answer frameworks;
- corrections to official links;
- accessibility and formatting improvements.

## Not accepted

- leaked or confidential questions;
- claims that a question is guaranteed to appear;
- copied commercial material;
- unverifiable interview-process claims;
- bulk low-quality question lists;
- promotional spam.

By contributing, you confirm that the submission is original or properly licensed.
