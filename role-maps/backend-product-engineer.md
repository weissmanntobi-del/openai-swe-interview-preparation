# Role Map: Backend or Product Engineer

## Highest-priority areas

1. Practical coding
2. API and data-model design
3. Product reliability
4. Databases and caching
5. Observability and incident response
6. Collaboration and ownership

## Strong evidence

- shipped user-facing systems;
- made sensible scope trade-offs;
- handled migrations safely;
- improved reliability or latency;
- worked effectively with product and design;
- owned production outcomes.
